Afif Al Mamun

PhD Student, University of Calgary

520B ICT Building

2500 University Drive NW

Calgary, AB, Canada T2N 1N4

I am Afif, a graduate student at the University of Calgary, supervised by Dr. Gias Uddin and working with IBM CAS Canada. I also serve as a Graduate Assistant in Research & Teaching at UCalgary. Alongside my studies, I am currently working as an intern at IBM Canada through Mitacs Accelerate. My current research centers on Large Language Models and Software Engineering, areas I am deeply passionate about. Before my graduate studies, I worked for several years as a Software Engineer specializing in AI. During this time, I had the opportunity to contribute to various cutting-edge projects across different domains. I was also honored with a gold medal upon receiving my Bachelor of Science degree.

Publications & Research Contributions

  • Hallucination Detection in Large Language Models with Metamorphic Relations
    Yang Borui, Md Afif Al Mamun, Jie Zhang, Gias Uddin
    Proceedings of the ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering (FSE), 2025.
    🏆 ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Award.
    This work introduces metamorphic relations to systematically detect hallucinations in LLMs, advancing reliability analysis for AI-assisted software engineering.

  • Evaluating Deep Learning Based Domain Generalization for Motion Mitigation in Multi-Center Brain MRI
    Saad Ashraf, Md Afif Al Mamun, Mumu Akter, Roberto Souza, Mariana Bento
    Reconstruction and Imaging Motion Estimation (RIME), 2025.
    Investigates domain generalization strategies to improve robustness of motion mitigation models across multi-center MRI datasets.

  • TLNet: A Deep CNN Model for Prediction of Tomato Leaf Diseases
    Md Afif Al Mamun, Dewan Ziaul Karim, Subroto Nag Pinku, Tasfia Anika Bushra
    International Conference on Computer and Information Technology (ICCIT), 2020.
    Proposes a convolutional neural network for accurate plant disease detection from leaf images.

  • Bangla Music Genre Classification Using Neural Network
    Md Afif Al Mamun, Imamul Kadir, AKM Shahariar Azad Rabby, Abdullah Al Azmi
    International Conference on System Modeling and Advancement in Research Trends (SMART), 2019.
    Presents a neural-network-based approach for genre classification in Bangla music.

    Md Afif Al Mamun (Undergraduate Thesis), 2020.
